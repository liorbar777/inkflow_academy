Our Story

Ink passed down
through living hands

Founded in Chengdu by Master Liú Wén — third-generation, Tang tradition — Tong Shu began as a courtyard studio and grew into Sichuan's most respected brush arts centre.

Calligraphy is not performance. It is daily practice. Small groups, lineage teachers, personal guidance from first stroke to finished work.
